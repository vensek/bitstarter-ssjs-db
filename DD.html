Advice from Doctors for Brain Development

According to Doctors, Parents who expose children to outdoor environment, contribute to the babies' cognitive senses opening up, and to the nurturing of brain development. The baby's experience with the external world forms the edifice on the foundation laid by its genes, and influences her learning ability as she grows. Parents must be conscious of the fact that even before the baby is born, she has several billions of brain cells that are required throughout her life. The growth of these cells is to some extent stimulated by the activity of nerve pathways leading to the brain. The greater the stimuli to these pathways through the baby's senses, the more will be the impetus to establishment of connections amongst the brain cells.

When your baby is exposed to an environment that is replete with colors, sound, smell, variety etc., the formation of the connections between the neurons (the brain cells) gets intensified. The cuddle that you give as a parent introduces the baby to the new environment in the backdrop of the comfort and security provided by your arms.
